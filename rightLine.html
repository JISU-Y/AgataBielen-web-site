Right Line

The line broken, bent and bonded in space, creates three-dimensional forms. This very simple statement on geometry and technique is one of Agata Bielen's elemental design principles. ANd the Poland based jewellery designer makes sure to come closer and closer to this principle twith every collection she creates. For Spring/Summer 2016 she focusses on the optical illusion created by fine lines running through space, more broken than bent or bonded. That's whty the collection bears the name Right Line.
